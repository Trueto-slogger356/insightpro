## 📊 Key features

Insightpro includes tools for professional survey management.

### Dynamic workflows
You can build surveys that change based on user answers. If a person chooses a specific option, the survey takes them to a related follow-up question. This keeps your surveys relevant and short.

### Live analytics
View your data as it comes in. The dashboard updates every time someone completes a survey. You can see trends, total responses, and completion rates in simple charts.

### User permissions
You control who sees your data. You can set up roles for different members of your team. Give some people view-only access, while others edit the surveys or change the settings.

### Reliable storage
The application saves your progress as you work. If you lose internet access or the power fails, the recovery system saves your session. You can pick up where you left off without losing any progress.

## 💡 Using the platform

When you open the application, log in with your credentials. If you do not have an account, click the sign-up button on the entry screen. 

### Creating a survey
Once you log in, go to the dashboard and press the "Create" button. Give your survey a name. Use the drag-and-drop tool to add questions. You can add text boxes, multiple-choice options, or rating scales.

### Sending your survey
After you finish the design, click "Publish." The system creates a unique link. You can send this link via email or post it on your website. Anyone with the link can take the survey.

### Reviewing results
Click the "Results" tab in your dashboard to see your data. The window displays current counts for every question. Export these reports as a file to share them with others.

## 🛡️ Data security

Your information stays private. The application uses encryption to hide your data while it travels over the web. We also use secure login tokens to confirm your identity.

## ❓ Frequently asked questions

### Can I run this on a Mac?
This version currently works only on Windows. 

### Will my existing surveys work?
Insightpro imports data from common survey formats. Use the import tool in settings to bring your files into the platform.

### How do I update the software?
The application checks for updates when you start it. If an update exists, click "Install" to receive the latest features and security fixes.

### What happens if I forget my password?
Click the "Forgot password" link on the login screen. We send a recovery code to your registered email address.

### Can I delete my data?
Yes. Go to the account settings and select "Delete all projects." This removes your surveys and the data you collected.
